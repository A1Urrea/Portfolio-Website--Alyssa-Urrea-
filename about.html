My Story

My name is Alyssa Urrea, I am currently enrolled in an online course with CareerFoundry studying UX design. I am a stay at home mom of three boys ages 14, 12 and 3 years old. My previous background I was a care giver to the elderly for over 10 years. Even though it was self rewarding at times I was never really happy. Over the 5 years of being a stay at home mom I have been researching new careers to start since it was a perfect time to do so. I did feel lost as I was trying to find what I want to do until I came across UX design. I started to do research on the subject to make sure this is something I wanted to do and as I continued researching the subject the more I became excited. I was able to envision myself in this role. I decided to take the leap and sign up for online course. With this new chapter in my life at age 35 it is very exciting to say I am starting a new career that I feel actual joy for. When I am not studying or doing all that mom stuff, I enjoy going out with my little family. I enjoy taking road trips, driving 2 hours NW to go to Santa Monica to enjoy the views, or 1.5 hours W to Huntington Beach to enjoy the weather, food and local breweries or 1.5 hours SW to visit San Diego and family. If it is just me I enjoy doing little crafts like making tumblers, pens, customizable t-shirts and cards.
